06 — Usage Guidelines

DO'S AND DON'TS

✓ Do
  • Use construction terminology naturally — RFI, submittal, punchlist, daily log
  • Position as companion/partner, never replacement
  • Lead with practical outcomes: time saved, drafts generated, context remembered
  • Keep "EBFC" and "AI" as two visual units — EBFC bold, AI in gold
  • Use dark backgrounds as primary — this is a premium product
  • Reference real construction workflows and pain points
  • Speak directly: "Your AI" not "An AI solution"
✕ Don't
  • Use Silicon Valley jargon: "disrupt," "leverage AI," "paradigm shift"
  • Show hard-hat stock photos or cliché construction imagery
  • Claim to replace human judgment or expertise
  • Use light/white as primary background — it breaks the premium feel
  • Separate "EBFC" from "AI" in the logo — they're one unit
  • Oversell capabilities — be honest about what the AI can and can't do
  • Use blue and gold in equal proportion — blue leads, gold accents
